Serenis

Beyond Green Tropical Living

Serenis

If you are in the market to buy a house or thinking about relocating in the North of Cebu, Consolacion and Liloan are the best choices. It's not too far from the main city and residential development is booming in the north. Serenis is the newest and an exciting development that you would surely consider. It's perfectly located in a calm highland in Yati, Liloan, but in close proximity to Consolacion.

Serenis is perfect for start up families like you. With a generous space to accommodate grandpa and grandma too.

Serenis: Green living

Here in SERENIS, homes are build with the tropical climate in mind. Clever design adjusts to and accommodate our year round sunny environment by using time tested principles in tropical living.

Serenis, is set back a few hundred meters from the highway and its pollution, nosie, dust and traffic. This spells a big difference in everyday life. Better sleep at night, clearner surroundings, safer environment. Serenity.

Location is Everything

Yati, Liloan

While location provides privacy and a pocket of serenity away from the main road, it is very near a mall, market, schools, a hospital, the parish church of Consolaction and everything you need without rushing to the city. Convenience in a big way.
