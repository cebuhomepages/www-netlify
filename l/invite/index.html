Location is Everything

Nested in the very accessible FE Zuellig Avenue, Mandaue City, Cebu, It occupies a waterfront site of approximately 20 hectares with a prominent location between Mactan Island, the home of Cebu International Airport, and Cebu business park, a major commercial area in Cebu. The project will be developed in phases over more than 10 years.

Parkmall and S&R, Cebu Doctors University and Singapore International School, UCMed and Chong Hua Hospital, all within full accessibility.

Master Planned for the Future

Mandani Bay will be primarily residential with retail and office components that will combine to form a master-planned urban community.Key features of the project are its strategic location and extensive water frontage.

Phase One will comprise two blocks of approximately 1,200 condominium units. In addition to typical studio to 3-bedroom units there will be a number of special units including loft units, garden duplexes, townhouses and penthouses. The project will also provide a range of leisure and recreational facilities as well as car parking.

Phase 1 is located close to the Mactan channel and many units will feature breathtaking sea views. 2 towers are scheduled for completion in 2020.

World Class Destination

The vision is to establish Mandaue as a desirable mixed-used hub of Cebu. A vision on par with other vibrant progressive cultural districts through out the world.

This can be attained by transforming the industrial & manufacturing district into a desirable lifestyle district.

There will be a Green Strip, this is inspired by New York's Central Park.. The Boulevard, a stretch along the main entrance, inspired by a famous street in Los Angeles. And of course, the Waterfront, inspired by the Marina Bay in Singapore.

Mandani Bay Suites | Twin Towers

Open now for reservations is Phase 1, Towers 1 and 2, dubbed as Mandani Bay Suites.

These two towers are estimated to be delivered Q1 of 2020. Unit owners will enjoy the first phase of the entire 20 hectare development.
